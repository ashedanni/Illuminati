About


Illuminati, is an annual inter college cultural and literary festival arranged by MCET. It is a launchpad for upcoming artists, a showcase of spectacle where every step you take is a step towards new experiences, friendships and memories are born.

Our mission is to give a chance to young guns to break new grounds on the cultural scene.

Embrace yourself for another cultural extravaganza. Join us this year from 2 November to 3rd November at Mohandas College of Engineering and Technology
